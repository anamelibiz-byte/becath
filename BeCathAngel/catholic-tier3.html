Angels & Your Guardian
What Scripture says, what they do, and how your guardian angel walks beside you every day.
What God Says About Angels
Angels Are Real — Sent by God
The Bible names angels over 300 times. They are spiritual beings God created before the world, pure intellect and will — not human souls who died. They are ministers of God's plan.
"Are they not all ministering spirits sent out to serve for the sake of those who are to inherit salvation?" — Hebrews 1:14
God Assigns You a Guardian
The Church teaches — and Jesus Himself confirmed — that each person has a guardian angel assigned from birth (some theologians say from conception), who accompanies you through your entire life.
"See that you do not despise one of these little ones. For I tell you that in heaven their angels always see the face of my Father who is in heaven." — Matthew 18:10
"For he will command his angels concerning you to guard you in all your ways." — Psalm 91:11
What Your Guardian Angel Does
🛡️
Protects You
Guards you from physical and spiritual dangers — including the attacks of demons and near occasions of sin.
🙏
Intercedes for You
Presents your prayers before God's throne and intercedes on your behalf, especially when you forget to pray.
💡
Inspires Your Mind
Illuminates your intellect with holy thoughts, promptings toward virtue, and reminders of God's presence.
⚠️
Warns of Danger
Sends interior warnings — that uneasy feeling, that sudden caution — to steer you from harm or sin.
📖
Records Your Life
Some theologians teach your guardian keeps a spiritual record of your life to present at your particular judgment.
🌿
Accompanies You to Death
At death, your guardian angel escorts your soul — just as Lazarus was carried by angels to Abraham's side (Luke 16:22).
The Three Named Archangels
⚔️
St. Michael
"Who is like God?"
Commander of the heavenly army. Defeated Lucifer in the war of heaven. Prince of Israel. Patron of the Church, soldiers, police, and the dying. Invoked for protection against Satan.
📜
St. Gabriel
"God is my strength"
Messenger of the Incarnation. Announced to Mary that she would bear the Son of God. Also appeared to Daniel and Zechariah. Patron of communications, messengers, and diplomats.
🌿
St. Raphael
"God heals"
Guided Tobias on his journey; drove away the demon Asmodeus. Revealed himself: "I am Raphael, one of the seven angels who stand in the glorious presence of the Lord." Patron of the sick, travelers, and the blind.

How to Work with Your Guardian Angel
Morning: Greet your guardian angel at the start of each day. Ask for protection and guidance.

Before decisions: Ask your angel to help you see clearly and choose well.

Before sleep: Ask your angel to guard your rest and bring you holy dreams.

October 2: Feast of the Guardian Angels — the Church's celebration of this doctrine.

Key insight: Your angel sees God face-to-face at all times (Mt 18:10) while remaining present to you. This is not a contradiction — it reflects the angelic nature of pure spirit.

Psalm 63 — Thirsting for God
A psalm of David when he was in the desert of Judah
O God, You are my God,
for You I long;
for You my soul is thirsting.
My body pines for You
like a dry, weary land without water.
So I gaze on You in the sanctuary
to see Your strength and Your glory.
For Your love is better than life,
my lips will speak Your praise.
So I will bless You all my life,
in Your name I will lift up my hands.
My soul shall be filled as with a banquet,
my mouth shall praise You with joy.
On my bed I remember You.
On You I muse through the night
for You have been my help;
in the shadow of Your wings I rejoice.
As morning breaks I look to You, O God, to be my strength this day.

Supabase Architecture
1. Auth (Email + Magic Link)
Supabase Auth handles user signup/login. Magic link (passwordless) is the best UX for a prayer app — no password to forget.

supabase.auth.signInWithOtp({ email })

Row-level security ensures each user can only read their own journal entries and preferences.
2. Database Schema
Tables:

profiles (id, email, is_pro, stripe_customer_id, created_at) journal_entries (id, user_id, content, prayer_type, created_at) prayer_streaks (user_id, streak_days, last_prayer_date) push_tokens (user_id, token, platform, active)
