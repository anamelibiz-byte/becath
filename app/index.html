Questions

Everything you want to know.

What makes TinaMaria different from Calm or Headspace?+
Calm and Headspace give you the same pre-recorded content as everyone else. TinaMaria delivers a session matched to your specific money block, intention, and where you are in your journey. And every personalized session is yours to keep and replay — it was made for you.
Does hypnosis actually work?+
Yes — when done properly. Hypnosis is a theta brainwave state where the subconscious becomes receptive to new beliefs. It's used in clinical settings for pain management, anxiety, and behavioral change with decades of research behind it. TinaMaria applies this to wealth identity reprogramming with weekly repetition — which is what actually creates neuroplastic change.
Why once a week and not every day?+
Because repetition of the same session is what creates the neural pathway — not novelty. You receive a new personalized session every Monday and replay it every day that week. By Friday it's in your body. Sunday you're ready for what's next. That rhythm is how real subconscious change is built.
I've tried manifestation before and it didn't work. Why is this different?+
Manifestation works at the conscious level — affirmations, vision boards, journaling. These help, but they don't reach the 95% of your brain that actually makes decisions. Hypnosis works at the subconscious level, in theta state, which is where your money programming lives. It's the difference between repainting the walls and changing the foundation.
Do I keep my sessions if I cancel?+
Yes. Every session generated for you is saved to your library. It's yours. Play it tonight, next week, next year. We believe that when you invest in your own reprogramming — even one session — that investment belongs to you.
What is The Queen tier and is $999 real?+

She chooses the length, the focus, the intention. Each one is added to The Becoming library the following month — so Queen members literally shape what the whole community receives. Private application only.
Is this only for entrepreneurs?+
No — though most members are women building something. TinaMaria works for anyone who wants to change their relationship with money: getting out of debt, stopping the cycle of earning and spending, feeling safe receiving, charging what they're worth, investing for the first time.
